About Us

We are Softball Addicts teaching up and coming players the game of softball. We offer all areas oon the field within our classes. For each area there is a beginner course, followed by an intermediate course, and topped with a advanced course. Every beginner class falls on Mondays and Tuesdays, intermediate classes are on Wednesdays and Thursdays, and lastly our advanced classes remain on Fridays and Saturdays. *all courses are 2 hours long*
